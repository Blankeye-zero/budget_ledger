# budget_ledger
A mini project of mine for further understanding the features of flutter.

Budget Ledger is a cross-Platform Mobile App that helps us keep track of our Expenditures.

## Visualizes purchases...

Budget Ledger presents the statistics of your purchases over the past week through a chart that further helps you 
to keep track of your expenses.
